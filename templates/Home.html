Welcome to Hotel Clement Prague

PRAGUE BOUTIQUE HOTEL IN OLD TOWN


Welcome to the official website of the Hotel Clement Prague!

Our 4-star Hotel Clement Prague is located only a few steps from the Vltava River and the Palladium shopping center and just a short distance from major historic

sites located in Prague's Old Town, but at the same time, lies in the heart of Peter’s quarters, aside from rush and hush of touristic routes. Enjoy the place where

locals live, with small corner shops and cozy restaurants, with people walking their dogs and jogging in the morning. Enjoy the real Prague!


Warm and friendly service, helpful staff, 76 comfortable rooms, rich breakfast and pleasant ambiance – those are the experiences our guests evaluate highly in

their reviews. Right in the city center, yet in a quiet neighborhood close to the park and river, our Prague boutique hotel offers the perfect base for exploring the Old

Town and relaxing after the rush of a busy day, always with a smile and warm welcome of our friendly receptionists.


At Hotel Clement Prague, we are proud to say we are friendly in many aspects:


Family-friendly: kids get special treatment in our hotel and they have a lovely playground in the park just opposite the hotel. We have extra beds, baby cribs,

family rooms and interconnected rooms available.


Disabled-people-friendly: we have a spacious, barrier-free room with a specially adjusted bathroom.


Nutrition-diet-friendly: we provide gluten- or lactose-free food for our breakfast when requested. An extra charge of €5 is applied.


Gay and lesbian-friendly: we welcome every guest without making exceptions.


Non-smoker-friendly: the entire interior of the hotel is absolutely non-smoking.



Smokers-friendly: on the open-air terrace, public balcony on the 5th floor and balconies of Superior rooms, smoking permitted.


Car-driver-friendly: we have a few parking places in an underground garage in the hotel and two on-street parking cards for the entire Prague 1 area.



Internet-fan-friendly: free and fast Wi-Fi in all areas and rooms of the hotel and a public computer with printer is available to all our guests.
