CENTRAL BOUTIQUE ACCOMMODATION IN PRAGUE


Our designers have carefully crafted air-conditioned hotel rooms at Hotel Clement Prague, designed to make you feel at home. The rooms are divided into different

categories, allowing everyone to find their favourite.


All our central Prague accommodation features very comfortable beds – either king or 2 twins, with full-size extra bed in triple rooms. All rooms are equipped with a

minibar, safety box, tea and coffee facilities, flat TV, bathrooms amenities, bath with shower or shower corner.

Should you look for space, we can offer you our spacious Junior Suite with seating and desk in small living room or our exclusive Superior Suite that offers two full

bathrooms, bedroom and living rooms equipped with 2 TV sets and Nespresso Coffee Machine. Both rooms can accommodate also one or two additional guests.


Freshly refurbished Superior rooms on the 7th, 8th and 9th floor feature warm and accommodating design in coffee and honey colors, soft and luxury fabrics, and

natural oak wooden details that will give you the feeling of utmost luxury and comfort together with additional amenities like Nespresso Coffee Machine, bathrobes

and slippers, sider choice of bathroom cosmetics and in addition complimentary water and fresh fruit on arrival.

In our top category, on our top floor, we present black-and-red design Superior Rooms with Balcony and Superior Rooms with Panoramic windows offering a

magnificent view of the river and Prague city. Whether you prefer romantic views or fresh air, you won’t be disappointed. It is hard to describe it, it needs to be

experienced…simply the best rooms to stay in Prague.
